Low Top Suede Shoes

Our original custom shoe, the low top suede, was the result of our design team's favorite things about shoes: from color, to design, to comfort, to use, to durability. These shoes are regarded as some of the more versatile every day shoes by our design team. Making our shoes in-house allows us to customize the shoes to the customer's needs, whether they need wide shoes, taller shoes, softer insoles, or harder insoles, we can curate those things. We aspire to have these shoes be the perfect shoe for general use, not one specific reason.
